Getting started — How-To Videos

Two short videos to help you get set up for the course before Week 1 assignments are due.

1

How to schedule an appointment with Dr. M. using Calendly

The Dr. M. 1-on-1 is a required part of the Sex in the Real World project, due by 5pm on Friday, June 19th. This video walks you through how to find an available time and book your appointment using Calendly. Don't wait until the last week — slots fill up fast in a 6-week course.

What the video covers

  • 1Opening the Calendly booking link from the syllabus
  • 2Selecting an available date and time that works for you
  • 3Entering your name and NSU email to confirm the appointment
  • 4What to expect in your confirmation email
Ready to book? Head to Dr. M.'s Calendly page: calendly.com/1005373940-nevadastate/stumtg. Book during Week 3 or early Week 4 — as soon as you've submitted your Project Description.
2

How to download and install Microsoft Word

Several assignments in this course require Word documents — including the Article Submission and Annotations and the Project Description. As an NSU student, you have free access to Microsoft Word through your student license. This video shows you how to download and install it.

What the video covers

  • 1Signing into your NSU student Microsoft account
  • 2Navigating to the Microsoft 365 download page
  • 3Downloading and installing Word on your computer
  • 4Opening Word and confirming your license is active
Already have Word? You're all set — no need to reinstall. If you're unsure whether your version is current, the video also shows you how to check.
